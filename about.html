Strong Coffee, Stronger Spirit of God About Our Cafe

Founded in 2022 by Roman Wang, our establishment has been serving up rich coffee sourced from artisan farmers in various regions of South and Central Provo. We are dedicated to travelling Utah, finding the best coffee, and bringing back to you here in our cafe.

We guarantee that you will convert to our decadent blends the moment you walk inside until you finish your last sip. Join us for your daily routine, an outing with the missionaries, or simply just to enjoy some alone time.
